Gutter Cleaning

Say goodbye to the leaves and debris clogging your drainage system. Paul’s Cleaners in Melbourne provide professional gutter and downpipe cleaning for domestic and commercial properties.  With our gutter cleaning service, your house will get back its healthy drainage system in no time.

Why clean your gutters?

Your gutters collect debris that’s rarely flushed out by rainfall. When the water can’t pass freely, due to them being blocked, it may overflow or backflow into the ceiling area. Homeowners often overlook their roof gutters until they cause problems. Blocked downpipes are another problem we are here to help you with. After our gutter cleaning service, your home’s gutter and downpipe system will be back in working order.By cleaning your gutters you prevent a lot of potential problems including:

  • Overflowing and leaking gutters
  • Flooded roof and basement
  • Cracked foundations
  • Mould
  • Insect infestation and bacterial outbreaks
  • Roof fire (caused by dry leaves)

Why Choose Paul’s Gutter Cleaning Services in Melbourne

  • Affordable and easy to book service
  • Experienced technicians that will properly clean your gutters and downspouts.
  • You won’t endanger yourself by climbing on ladders and trying to do the cleaning.
  • Save on costly repairs and prolong the life of your house.

Regular gutter cleaning will prevent property damage. Gutter cleaning should be done at least twice per year, depending on whether or not you have trees around your house. By doing this, you will reduce the probability of water damage to your property and critters nesting in your draining system.
