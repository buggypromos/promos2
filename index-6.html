Our End of Lease Cleaning Checklist:

General/ Living areas/ Bedrooms:Vacuum and mop floors; Wipe skirting boards; Clean built-in cabinets/wardrobes Clean all windows (internally); Wipe window sills (internally), clean tracks; Wipe doors/door frames and light switches; Remove cobwebs Dust blinds; Dust light fittings; Clean railings

Kitchen:Clean all cupboards inside and out; Wipe and polish all surfaces and benches; Deep cleaning of the oven (in & out); Clean stovetop, hood, splashback; Vacuum and mop the floor

Bathroom/ Toilets:Clean all cupboards (inside & out); Clean toilets; De-lime and descale shower area – shower screen & tiles; De-lime & descale wall and floor tiles; Clean and polish sink, vanity and surfaces; Clean bowl; Clean bathtub; Exhaust fans/vent – wipe and dust-free; Clean mirrors

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Do you provide the cleaning materials and equipment?

    A: Yes. The complete set of equipment and materials. All we would need is electricity & running water.

  • Q: How many cleaners will be sent to my home?

    A: Most of our cleaning teams consist of 2-3 cleaners, however, for larger properties we can arrange a bigger team. We always ensure that the job is completed on time and you get your full bond back.

    A: We will thoroughly vacuum your carpets as part of the service. Upon request, we can also professionally clean them using the hot water extraction method. Take advantage of our great discounts when combining the End of Lease Cleaning with our Carpet cleaning service.
