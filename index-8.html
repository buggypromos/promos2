Oven Cleaning

Paul’s Professional Oven & BBQ Cleaners are here to restore your cooking appliances back to their best.

Oven and Barbecue cleaning can be very exhausting and time-consuming. Not only does it require a lot of effort, but also specialised equipment.

The cleaners are extremely professional and have years of experience. They carry the appropriate cleaning solutions needed to effectively take care of your cooking appliances.

Don’t waste your time. Leave the dirty job to us!

Benefits of Booking Paul’s Oven & BBQ Cleaning:

  • It will prolong the life of your Oven or BBQ
  • Minimises the risk of burning your food
  • Decreases energy consumption as a dirty oven takes longer time to heat up
  • We use Eco-friendly and non-caustic detergents
  • We are available 7 days per week with no extra charge on weekends and holidays
  • Combine the oven cleaning service with one-off/spring cleaning or carpet cleaning and receive great discounts

How we do it:

When the professional technician arrives on site, he will first inspect the oven or bbq. Making sure it’s working properly. Then place a protective sheet underneath, ensuring that your floors and surrounding areas are protected. After that, he will disassemble all parts removable parts - racks and trays. Soak them in a tank with a powerful solution that dissolves the burnt-on grease and grime. Killing all germs, and bacteria. The internal oven will be manually scrubbed. If the grill element can be pulled down, the technician will clean the oven ceiling too. After everything is cleaned properly, he will assemble all parts back together. The outside of the oven, as well as all knobs, will be cleaned and polished. The technician will finally test the oven to check if it is working properly. You will be able to use it right after the service.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How long will it take to clean my oven?

    A: Depending on the type, size and condition of the oven the service could take between 1-2 hours.

  • Q: Do you clean commercial ovens?

    A: Yes, we do. We can provide you with a quote after organizing a viewing.

  • Q: How often should I have my oven cleaned?

    A: It is recommended to have your oven professionally cleaned every 6-12 months, depending on how often it has been used.

  • Q: When can I use my oven?

    A: You can use the oven right after the service, however it is recommended that you turn it on at high temperature for 10-15 minutes before using it.
