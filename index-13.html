Tile and Grout Cleaning

Tile and Grout Cleaning that will make you say “Wow”.
Occasional mopping and sweeping of your tiled floors may not be enough to keep them in their top condition.
This is where we step in. No more blackened grout! Paul’s Cleaners are here to make your hard floors shine again.

How We Do It:

Our Melbourne-based tile and grout cleaning will make your hard floor shine.

  1. To start off with, the cleaning experts of Paul’s Cleaning Melbourne will prepare the area for Tile and grout cleaning.
  2. Afterwards, we will closely inspecting your floor for loose or deformed tiles.
  3. Then it will be treated with specialised cleaning agents.
  4. After that, we will professionally clean the floor with the high-pressure spinner, producing the astonishing 1000 to 1200 PSI. The used detergents will remove all dirt that has built up over time on your tiled floor.
  5. Furthermore, the technicians will be extra thorough in their job, paying special attention to all stained areas and dirty patches.

Types of surfaces we clean:

Ceramic, Porcelain, Natural Stone, and others. If you're not certain what's the type of surface you want us to clean, simply contact our customer service. The agents will be more than happy to assist you.

Benefits of Booking Paul’s Professional Tile and Grout Cleaning

  • Restores the good look of your tiled floors
  • Prolongs the lifespan of your hard floor
  • Improves the look and value of the property
  • Prevent cracks in your grout and reduce the risk of mould build-up
  • Keeps your tile and grout surfaces free from germs and bacteria
