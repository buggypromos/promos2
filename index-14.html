High Pressure Cleaning

If you want your outdoor surfaces really restored to their original look, pressure cleaning them is the way to go. The team will bring back the cleanliness of your driveways, paths, patios, stairs and walls.

Paul’s high-pressure cleaning experts have years of cleaning experience and regular training on top of it. Combine that with their professional equipment and the results are proven:

  • Even the most difficult to reach places cleaned to a sparkle
  • Surfaces are perfectly washed and undamaged
  • A cleaning service that does wonders for all kinds of exterior surfaces
  • Environmentally-friendly method, using only water
  • An expert service with no additional charges

High-Pressure Cleaning is Fast and Spectacularly Efficient

The method the jet washing experts use involves a high-pressure mechanical sprayer, which makes the cleaning process quite simple and quick, but at the same time guarantees excellent results. That’s why the service will work just perfectly for abrasive surfaces, like driveways and brick walls.

It takes just seconds for the experts to set up the equipment. The professional cleaners simply plug the sprayer into the water tap and are ready to clean. The mechanical washer produces powerful pressure (from 750 to 2000 PSI), which is sure to cleanse deep into any surface, leaving it jaw-droppingly polished. You can depend on us to easily take care of:

    Dirt and mud
  • Oil and petroleum products
  • Loose paint
  • Mould and mildew

Plus, the brick cleaning team will be out of your hair in no time, leaving your property in pristine condition.
