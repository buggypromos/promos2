Initially, Paul started with an idea to provide a full range of domestic services suitable for all types of properties in the Melbourne. Driven by the passion to give customers more than just cleaning, he soon built a reputation of a leading service provider.

As the years passed and Paul’s cleaning business grew more and more successful, the idea of looking for partners was born.
