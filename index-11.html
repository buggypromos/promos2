After Builders

We make newly build properties shine!
Paul’s Cleaners in Melbourne will ensure that, after a construction or renovation, your property is spotless.
Our company is here to take care of your dust, rubble, and debris in an efficient and professional matter. We have been doing this for over a decade and we know that details matter. Trust the experienced cleaners!

The After Builders Service Includes

A free viewing of the property will be organised, in order to give you a proper quote. Followed by:

Professional cleaning of all internal areas - kitchen, living rooms, bedrooms, laundry areas, hallways, staircases, etc.
Deep cleaning of bathrooms, toilets, bathtubs, showers, exhaust fans, etc.
Deep cleaning of all skirting boards, wainscoting, doors, door frames, fixtures, switches and sockets.
All surfaces and woodwork will be polished.
Deep cleaning of all hard floors, railings and balconies.
Professional cleaning of all windows (in and out). As well as cleaning of all tracks, sills and ledges, window-frames.
All the dirt and dust will be removed. As well as unsightly markings left by paint or plaster.
We ensure a healthy, dust free property.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Do I have to provide the cleaning materials?

    A: No, the team takes care of this and it is included in the final price of the service.

  • Q: Can I be provided with a quote over the phone?

    A: We can only give you an estimate over the phone. After a free viewing has been organized we will be able to give you a final quote. In some cases, if the renovation was on a small scale, we may be able to provide you with a quote over the phone that would be based on your description of the workload.

  • Q: Can I book After-builders cleaning if the builders are not done with the work yet?

    A: We recommend the cleaning to be carried out after the builders have finished their work. The reason for this is that we can’t ensure the best possible results if there will be more construction/renovation work taking place while we are cleaning or after the cleaning.

  • Q: Is carpet cleaning included in the service?

    A: Yes. Your carpets will be vacuumed with an industrial strength vacuum cleaner. If you require a deep clean for your carpeting (steam cleaning), this can be organised for you at an additional price.
